Connecticut Real Estate, Fiscal & Demographic Analysis

A comprehensive exploration of how housing prices, municipal finances, and population trends have evolved across Connecticut's towns over the past two decades.

Project Overview

This project investigates patterns in Connecticut's real estate transactions, fiscal indicators, and demographic shifts from 2001 to 2040. Using publicly available datasets, we aim to identify how property values, taxation capacity, and population dynamics interact to shape the housing and economic landscape of the state. The findings are intended to support data-driven policy analysis and spatial decision-making.

Datasets and Sources

  • Real Estate Sales (2001–2022): Property transaction data collected from the Connecticut Open Data Portal.
    Source: CT Open Data Portal
  • Municipal Fiscal Indicators (2019–2024): Economic and grand list data by municipality, provided by the Office of Policy and Management (OPM).
    Source: OPM / CT Open Data Portal
  • Town Population Projections (2015–2040): Long-term demographic forecasts from the Connecticut State Data Center.
    Source: CT State Data Center
